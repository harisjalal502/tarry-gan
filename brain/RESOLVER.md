# Tarry Office Brain Resolver

This brain source stores physical-room context captured by Tarry.

## Filing Rules

1. Meeting-like conversations go in `meetings/`.
2. Raw room/event ledgers go in `sources/physical-room/`.
3. Whiteboard observations stay attached to the relevant meeting until they become durable ideas, projects, or concepts.
4. Generated pages should preserve speaker labels and source timing when available.
