# Tarry Submission Video Script

Target length: 90 seconds. Hard cap: 2 minutes.

## Core Thesis

The best startup context does not live in docs. It happens in the room.

Tarry is the office companion that sees people, reacts in the room, and turns physical context into searchable GBrain memory.

## Cut

### 0:00-0:07 - Cold Open

Black screen. Real office audio starts before the image.

Voiceover:

> So many ideas die in the air.

Cut to a real office. Two teammates are debating at a desk or whiteboard. Reachy Mini is visible.

Voiceover:

> Not because they were bad. Because they happened in the office.

### 0:07-0:22 - The Missing Layer

Show Slack/docs/calendar/GitHub quickly on a laptop, then cut back to the room.

Voiceover:

> Slack captures messages. Docs capture polished thoughts. Calendar captures meetings. But the room still has no memory.

### 0:22-0:45 - Tarry Sees And Reacts

Show Reachy looking at people. Show dashboard robot POV with live camera and face boxes.

On-screen labels:

- Person detected.
- Current speaker.
- Robot attention.
- Reaction triggered.

Voiceover:

> Tarry watches the room, detects who is present, and makes its attention visible by moving and reacting.

### 0:45-1:05 - Tarry Remembers

Show transcript, context cards, and memory writes.

On-screen labels:

- Decision detected.
- Risk unresolved.
- Whiteboard context.
- Written to GBrain.

Voiceover:

> It turns the physical office into structured memory: decisions, risks, questions, and whiteboard context.

### 1:05-1:22 - Retrieval

Show someone typing or asking:

> Prep me for the investor meeting. What did we decide about pricing?

Show GBrain retrieval answer.

Voiceover:

> Later, the team can search what happened in the room, the same way they search digital context.

### 1:22-1:30 - Close

Optional fast SF/office/world zoom or office montage. Do not overdo the generated footage.

Voiceover:

> Now imagine this in every startup office in San Francisco. Then every office in the world.

Final card:

> Tarry
> The physical context layer for teams.

## Backup Lines

If the final line feels too big:

> Every team has a digital brain. Tarry gives the office a memory.

If we want more founder energy:

> The future of company memory starts in the room.
